Hosts page

Host Nations

This page tracks where each Rugby World Cup was staged. It gives the project a stronger historical backbone and creates space for future venue, travel, and regional analysis.

Shared hosts are included as they were used in the tournament, so the archive can grow naturally into a fuller event history later.
